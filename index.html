About
Padmagiri Creation.

We are a sub-branch of a 100+ years old company with our main branch in Varanasi/Banaras, dedicated to provide high-quality fabrics and textiles to our client. Our company was founded in 2010 with a vision to offer a wide range of fabric solutions to clients across various locations. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a reliable supplier of premium quality fabrics and textiles.

We believe that the fabric you wear should be a reflection of your personality and style. That's why we offer a wide range of Dress Materials, Sarees, Lenghas, and Dupattas that cater to different tastes and preferences, from traditional to modern designs. Whether you're a designer, retailer, or individual customer, we have a wide range of options to suit your needs. From luxurious silks and soft cottons to durable technical textiles, we offer a diverse selection of fabrics and textiles to help bring your vision to life.

Thank you for considering our company for your fabric and textile needs. With a century of experience behind us, we look forward to the opportunity to work with you and provide you with the best fabrics and textiles in the industry.
